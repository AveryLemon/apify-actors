# P2-4: Cross-Modal Brand Analysis Actor

## Goal
Build an Apify actor that analyzes a brand's visual AND audio content and generates a comprehensive brand consistency report.

## Why

- Unique: NO OTHER ACTOR on Apify combines visual + audio analysis
